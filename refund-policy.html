1. Order Cancellation

You may cancel an equipment order free of charge at any time before it is shipped. Once an order is marked as shipped it can no longer be cancelled, but it may still be eligible for return under Section 2. Same-day delivery orders can be cancelled only within 60 minutes of placing the order, as dispatch begins immediately.

To cancel, contact us with your order number (shown at checkout and in your confirmation) via the details in Section 7. Cancelled prepaid orders are refunded in full — including delivery fees, taxes and platform fees — to the original payment method.

2. Returns

Most equipment can be returned within 7 days of delivery, provided it is unused, in its original packaging, and with all tags, manuals and accessories intact. To start a return, contact us with your order number and the reason for return. Once the returned item passes inspection, your refund is processed as per Section 3.

Return shipping costs are borne by the customer, except where the return is due to our error (damaged, defective or incorrect item — see Section 4), in which case we arrange and pay for pickup.

3. Refund Timelines

  • Approved refunds are initiated within 2 business days of approval.
  • Refunds are made to the original payment method via Razorpay and typically reflect in 5–7 business days, depending on your bank.
  • For returns, the goods value and taxes are refunded; original delivery fees are refunded only when the return is due to our error.

4. Damaged, Defective or Wrong Items

If an item arrives damaged, defective, or is not what you ordered, report it within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the item and packaging. We will arrange a free pickup and, at your choice, ship a replacement or issue a full refund (including all fees).

5. Non-Returnable Items

  • Bowstrings, arrows and target faces that have been used or shot.
  • Items custom-cut, custom-fletched or custom-built to your specification.
  • Safety equipment (tabs, arm guards, chest guards) where hygiene seals have been opened.

6. Tournament Registrations

Tournament entry fees follow the cancellation terms of each event. Unless an event page says otherwise: cancellations up to 7 days before the event receive a full refund; within 7 days of the event, entry fees are non-refundable but may be transferred to another eligible athlete with organiser approval. If an event is cancelled by the organiser or by us, entry fees are refunded in full.

7. How to Request a Refund

Write to us at karateacademyin@gmail.com or use the contact form / on-site chat, quoting your order number. We respond to refund requests within 2 business days.
