⚠️ WADA Prohibited List applies to all Archery.Services registered athletes
Archery falls under the WADA Code. Beta-blockers (e.g. propranolol, atenolol) are prohibited in-competition. Athletes are strictly liable — "I didn't know" is not a defence. Always check with your physician and the Global Drug Reference Online (GDRO) before taking any medication.
