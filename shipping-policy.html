1. Where We Deliver

We ship PAN-India to all serviceable pincodes via our courier partners. Enter your pincode at checkout — if a pincode is temporarily unserviceable, checkout will let you know before payment. We do not currently ship internationally.

2. Delivery Charges

  • Standard delivery: CHF 49 per order, FREE on orders of CHF 999 or more.
  • Same-day delivery: CHF 149 per order (select metro pincodes — see Section 4).
  • All charges are shown item-by-item at checkout before you pay — goods, delivery, taxes and platform fee are each listed separately. No hidden charges after payment.

3. Delivery Timelines

  • Metros (Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune): 2–4 working days.
  • Other cities and towns: 3–6 working days.
  • Remote and North-East pincodes: 5–9 working days.

Orders are dispatched within 1–2 business days of payment confirmation. Large or heavy items (bows, target stands, mats) may need an extra 1–2 days for protective packing — archery equipment travels in reinforced packaging to arrive competition-ready.

4. Same-Day Super-Fast Delivery

For select metro pincodes, orders placed before 12:00 noon with the same-day option are dispatched immediately and delivered the same day. Orders placed after noon with this option are delivered the next morning. Availability is confirmed at checkout based on your pincode; if same-day is not available for your address, the option will not be charged.

5. Tracking & Delivery Attempts

Once your order ships you receive the courier and tracking details by email/SMS. Couriers make up to 3 delivery attempts; after that the shipment returns to us and we contact you to reschedule (a re-shipping fee may apply). Please ensure someone is available to receive high-value equipment, which may require an OTP or signature on delivery.

6. Delays & Lost Shipments

Timelines can occasionally be affected by weather, strikes or courier disruptions; we'll keep you informed if your order is delayed. If a shipment is confirmed lost in transit, we ship a free replacement or refund you in full — your choice. Damaged-on-arrival items are covered under our Refund & Cancellation Policy.

Questions about a delivery? Write to karateacademyin@gmail.com or use the on-site chat with your order number.
